# Selleo Case Studies

Selleo has delivered over 200 software products since 2005 for clients across EdTech, HRM, FinTech, Healthcare, Real Estate, E-commerce, and SaaS. This document summarizes selected Selleo case studies grouped by industry, with the key facts, outcomes, and a link to the full case study on selleo.com. Selleo's own product, Mentingo, is documented separately in [MENTINGO.md](./MENTINGO.md).

## EdTech

### Defined Careers (Defined Learning)

Defined Careers is a K-12 college and career readiness platform used by schools and districts across the United States. Selleo built the MVP from scratch in Ruby on Rails and React, then integrated it into the wider Defined ecosystem and created a dedicated design system for consistency at scale.

- Client location: USA
- Industry: K-12 EdTech, college and career readiness
- Results: 600+ career-connected projects, 400+ careers across 79 pathways and 16 clusters, 1,000+ districts in the wider Defined ecosystem, around 2 million live users
- Services: e-learning development, UX/UI, custom software, QA
- Stack: Ruby on Rails, React
- Case study: https://selleo.com/portfolio/online-learning-platform

### Skumani

Skumani is an EdTech platform for 7th and 8th grade students in Poland, built with the Żak Center for Science and Business to level access to quality extracurricular education. Selleo built the full ecosystem on three pillars: premium content, AI-driven personalization, and gamification, delivering the MVP in four months.

- Client location: Poland
- Industry: EdTech, primary education
- Results: MVP delivered in 4 months, 3 AI-powered teachers built on a RAG architecture, 300+ lessons and quizzes, 900+ educational formats
- Key features: dedicated AI Teachers, gamification with daily streaks, XP, and badges, a proprietary CMS, an interactive test engine with LaTeX support, and intelligent video lessons with a Moments system
- Services: e-learning, custom software, QA
- Case study: https://selleo.com/portfolio/skumani

- Case study: https://selleo.com/portfolio/multi-agent-ai-platform

### Qstream

Qstream is a mobile-first microlearning application for corporate training, built on spaced repetition and scenario-based learning to improve knowledge retention. Selleo delivered UX/UI, scalable admin flows, and advanced analytics, and explored AI-powered content authoring.

- Client location: USA
- Industry: corporate L&D, EdTech
- Results: up to 170% higher knowledge retention versus traditional training, around 93% average learner engagement, method validated in 20+ randomized controlled trials
- Stack note: significant TypeScript codebase, 3+ years of collaboration
- Services: e-learning, UX/UI, custom software, QA
- Case study: https://selleo.com/portfolio/microlearning-software
