## How Selleo works with clients

Selleo offers two main engagement models:

- Staff Augmentation: vetted engineers integrate into an existing in-house team to add capacity and expertise.
- Dedicated Development Teams: a cross-functional team owns the product end to end.

Selleo works in English from a single location, with time zone overlap suited to clients in Europe and North America. A free two-week developer trial is available to validate fit before a longer engagement.
