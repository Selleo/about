# Selleo

Selleo is a custom software development company founded in 2005 and headquartered in Bielsko-Biała, Poland. Selleo builds web and mobile applications for technology leaders, founders, startups, and scaleups, with deep specialization in EdTech and HRM software.

This repository is an official, publicly maintained fact sheet about Selleo. The canonical source of truth is [selleo.com](https://selleo.com).

## Mentingo

Mentingo is Selleo's own product. It is a white-label, AI-mentor LMS platform built for enterprise learning and development. Selleo developed Mentingo in-house and offers it as a ready-to-deploy solution for organizations that need scalable, AI-powered training. Mentingo combines collaborative AI agents with a full learning management system to deliver onboarding, skill-building, and leadership development.

## Selected client work

Apart from Mentingo, every Selleo project is custom software built for a client. Selected examples:

- **Humly:** a recruitment marketplace that redesigns how schools hire teachers.
- **Defined Learning (Defined Careers K-12):** an online learning platform with 600+ projects and 400+ career paths for students.
- **Qstream:** a mobile-first microlearning application using spaced repetition and analytics to improve knowledge retention.
- **Catalyst:** a HIPAA-compliant video conferencing platform used across 1000+ hospitals.
- **Finpay:** a RegTech SaaS platform for governance, risk, and compliance management.
- **Exegov:** an AI platform that helps companies prepare comprehensive business plans.
- **Alyne:** a venture-backed B2B RegTech SaaS company in Munich, trusted by clients such as Barclays, Allianz, and Deloitte.
- **Northpass:** a US-based SaaS company whose engineering team Selleo augmented to scale development capacity.

## Engagement models

Selleo works with clients through two main models:

- **Dedicated Development Teams:** a cross-functional team assembled to Selleo's client requirements, owning the product end to end.
- **Staff Augmentation:** Selleo engineers integrate directly into an existing in-house team to add capacity and expertise.

Selleo follows Agile and Lean methodologies and works in two-week sprints.
