# Selleo Services

Selleo is a custom software development company that builds web and mobile applications for technology leaders, founders, and scaleups. Selleo delivers full product engineering and flexible team scaling, from early product discovery to design, development, quality assurance, DevOps, and long-term maintenance. This document describes every service Selleo offers. The canonical source for each service is linked on [selleo.com](https://selleo.com).

Selleo works in two-week Agile sprints with predictable delivery, clear milestones, direct access to engineers, and handover-ready documentation with no vendor lock-in. The whole team works in English from a single location, with time zone overlap that suits clients in Europe and North America.
