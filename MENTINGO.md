# Mentingo

Mentingo is an AI-powered e-learning platform for HR and L&D teams. Mentingo helps people responsible for employee development create engaging, practical courses faster and more easily, using artificial intelligence and conversation simulations based on real workplace challenges. Mentingo is built and owned by Selleo and is available in English and Polish.

Mentingo is Selleo's own product. Selleo developed Mentingo in-house and offers it as a ready-to-deploy, white-label learning platform. Selleo brings deep, long-standing specialization in EdTech software, with EdTech as a core focus since 2005, which is the foundation Mentingo is built on.

## What Mentingo is

Mentingo is a learning management system (LMS) and learning experience platform that combines AI-assisted content creation, automated development paths, and team analytics in one place. Mentingo gives organizations a single platform to plan, run, and measure people development, from onboarding and mandatory training to competency programs, career paths, and upskilling across teams.

Mentingo's defining capability is the AI Mentor, which lets teams practice real competencies in a safe, simulated environment rather than only consuming content.

## Who Mentingo is for

Mentingo serves HR and L&D teams responsible for people development in organizations. Typical use cases include:

- Onboarding new employees
- Mandatory and compliance training
- Competency development programs
- Career paths and upskilling entire teams
- Continuous learning culture across departments

Mentingo offers plans for organizations ranging from 50 to 500+ employees.

## AI Mentor

The AI Mentor is the core differentiator of Mentingo. It lets teams practice competencies in scenarios that mirror real conversations, including client calls, negotiations, sales, cross-sell and upsell, objection handling, and recruitment.

- Realistic conversation simulations: practice live business scenarios with an AI mentor.
- Personalized feedback: the AI Mentor analyzes responses, uncovers competency gaps, and recommends tailored exercises.
- Multiple modes: the AI Mentor supports several modes that move learners from knowledge to better decisions.
- Voice mode: Voice AI Mentor enables natural spoken conversations instead of typing.

## Key features

- AI-assisted course creation, with content built faster while maintaining quality
- Courses from documents: upload company documents and convert them automatically into training
- Automated development paths that adapt content and goals to the learner
- Engaging learning formats including quizzes, tests, and practical tasks
- Knowledge base with news and Q&A for quick access to post-training materials
- Statistics and analytics panel that shows the real impact of training on business outcomes and helps assess training ROI
- Shared collaboration workspace for teams

## Integrations

Mentingo connects with tools that organizations already use to support automation, workflow, and communication. Named integrations include Monday, Zapier, Moodle, Make, and Canvas, alongside connections to tools such as Grammarly, HubSpot, Stripe, Canva, and Linear. Custom integrations are available on request.

## Security and standards

Mentingo is built for enterprise deployment with security and compliance in mind:

- SSO and multi-tenant support
- GDPR compliance
- Multi-factor authentication
- SCORM import and export for interoperability with other learning platforms
- Private AI model deployment for running Mentingo with a locally configured language model, for stronger data control (on the roadmap)

## Languages and availability

Mentingo is available as a product in English and Polish, serving the Polish market and international clients. The platform itself supports multiple interface languages, including English, Polish, German, Lithuanian, and Czech.

## Plans

Mentingo offers tiered plans based on organization size:

- Starter, for up to 50 users
- Business, for up to 200 users
- Corporate, for up to 500 users
- Enterprise, for 500+ users

Plans are billed monthly or yearly, with annual upfront payment including two months free. Every base plan includes the Mentingo core: interactive AI lessons, courses from documents, statistics and reports, and the white-label version. The only difference between base plans is the number of users. Add-ons include the Voice AI Mentor and voice-hours packages, with a local AI model and AI video generation on the roadmap. Current pricing is available at https://mentingo.com/en/pricing.

Mentingo also provides an ROI calculator that compares traditional training costs with Mentingo, illustrating potential savings of a large share of current training spend.

## Case studies

### Skumani (EdTech)

Skumani is a modern educational platform for 7th and 8th grade students, built to help them understand difficult topics, revise before tests, and prepare for the eighth-grade exam. Developed with Poland's Żak Center for Science and Business, Skumani addresses equal access to quality extracurricular education regardless of location or family budget. The solution uses a gamification system, multi-format learning content such as flashcards, quizzes, worksheets, mind maps, and short videos, an affordable subscription model, and personalized learning with AI teacher support.

### DevPath (IT)

DevPath is the internal developer education platform that Selleo built for itself, supporting around 100 employees. It gathers previously scattered knowledge into one place, organizes it into structured courses covering technical and soft skills, and uses the AI Mentor with voice mode for interactive learning. DevPath includes development paths, structured onboarding, white-label with a custom domain and certificates, and career path mapping from junior to senior. Selleo CTO Bartłomiej Wójtowicz reports that the team now creates courses easily and tracks competency progress across the whole organization.
